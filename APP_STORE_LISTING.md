## Description (4000 chars max)

```
Bakarat is the app that keeps score for the Baccarat 3-boards card game — around a real table with physical cards, or online with cards dealt virtually by the app.

TWO WAYS TO PLAY

• Counter — you play with real cards, the app tracks the rounds and calculates the debts. Create a counter, add your players, play, validate. Balances update automatically.

• Online — create a game or join via a 4-character code. Cards are dealt virtually, announcements and tie-breaks handled by the app.

THE ACCOUNT BOOK

Once the night ends, the Accounts tab shows exactly who owes you what (and who you owe). Debts are computed with a minimization algorithm (Tricount / Splitwise style) that reduces the number of transactions to the strict minimum.

When someone pays you back, tap "Paid" — the line is crossed out on both sides. No real money flows through the app: you settle between friends however you want.

SIMPLE SHARING

Create a counter, share the invitation link via Messages or WhatsApp. Your players open the link, pick their seat, and their history starts building. No account yet? No problem — a temporary guest account is enough to get started.

PRIVACY FIRST

No ads, no trackers. Your data stays yours (and on Supabase, hosted in Europe). No data is ever sold. You can delete your account at any time.

WHAT'S IN BAKARAT

• Sign in with Apple or email
• Temporary guest account (no email required)
• Full history, real-time balances
• Online mode with announcements, tie-breaks, full board
• Short invitation code (4 chars) for online
• Deep link to share a counter in one tap
• Auto sync across devices

Bakarat is designed for players who want to focus on the game, not on arithmetic.
```
