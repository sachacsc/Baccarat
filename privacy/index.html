Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Bakarat is an iOS app that helps you keep score for the Baccarat 3-boards card game, in person around a table or online via virtual cards. This policy explains plainly what data we collect, where it lives, and your rights.

What data we collect

  • Account — your email (or your Apple identifier if you use Sign in with Apple) and a username. If you choose the temporary guest mode, we only store an anonymous unique identifier (no email).
  • Profile picture — optional. Stored encrypted in your private bucket.
  • Games — the rounds (manches) you play: scores, board winners, per-player deltas. Used to display your debts and history.
  • Debts — who owes you, who you owe. Bilateral "paid" markings between players.
  • Notification token — an APNs (Apple Push) identifier if you opt in. No personal data attached.

What we don't collect

No ads, no third-party trackers (Facebook, Google Analytics, etc.). No location data. No phone contacts. No real money is ever exchanged in-app — Bakarat only keeps score between friends.

Where your data lives

Everything is hosted on Supabase (EU region, West Europe). Communications are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+). Your games and balances are protected by Row-Level Security policies on Postgres: another user can only see games they directly participated in.

Photos

When you pick a profile photo through the iOS picker, Bakarat only receives the selected image — never your full library. The image is resized to 512×512 then uploaded to your private bucket on Supabase Storage.

Sharing between players

When another player joins your counter via an invitation link, they see that game only and the debts involving the two of you. They do not see your other games or your other relationships.

Your rights

  • Sign out at any time from the Profile tab.
  • Request complete deletion of your account and all your data by emailing sacha.csc@gmail.com. Effective within 30 days.
  • Request a copy of all your data in a readable format.
  • Correct any inaccurate data.

Cookies / tracking

The iOS app uses no cookies or advertising tracking technology. No IDFA is requested. Bakarat does not perform user profiling.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top shows the current version. Substantial changes will be notified in-app.
